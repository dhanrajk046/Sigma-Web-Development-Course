Train Ticket Reservation

Train ticket reservation (IRCTC) is simple: first create an IRCTC account and log in on the IRCTC website or Rail Connect app, then enter your “From” and “To” stations, travel date, class, and quota (General/Tatkal), search for trains, check availability (Available/RAC/WL), select a suitable train, fill passenger details like name, age, and berth preference, and finally pay using UPI/card/netbanking; after successful payment you’ll receive your e-ticket with a PNR number, which you should save for the journey.
